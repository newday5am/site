The Forum

Al-Anon’s monthly magazine The Forum features timely sharings from Al-Anon and Alateen members, suggested meeting topics, and the latest information on worldwide Al-Anon recovery. Experience and insight you won’t find anywhere else!

Click here to view recent articles and to subscribe to The Forum magazine.

The Forum Needs Your Sharings about Our Three Legacies! Please consider writing about one of the Twelve Steps, Twelve Traditions, or Twelve Concepts of Service, as your World Service Office receives very few sharings about them. Submissions should range from between 200 and 400 words.

Ther are three ways you can submit your articles:

  • Mail your share to The Forum, 1600 Corporate Landing Parkway, Virginia Beach, VA 23454-5617, OR

  • Email your share to wso@alanon.org, OR

  • You may click here to submit your share directly to The Forum. This site contains writing guidelines and a convenient form to submit your share.

A special thanks to our Alternate-GR and GR for keeping our Group informed about our monthly magazine The Forum

Member Blog

Al-Anon has a blog where members can share their experiences. New topics are added each month. As always you may write about Al-Anon’s three legacies. Each month features the Step, Tradition, and Concept of the month. For example the month of May will feature Step 5, Tradition 5, and Concept 5.
