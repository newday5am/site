About the Group Business Meeting

The Group Business Meeting / Group Conscience is held during our regular meeting on the last day of each month from 5:45AM to 6AM.

The Group Conscience is the result of the Group’s Business Meeting discussions. The Group Conscious is the will of the Group. The guiding principles for the Group Conscience are always the 12 Traditions and the 12 Concepts of Service (please see 2022-2025 Al-Anon / Alateen Service Manual, p. 48-49).

Please join us!

All members are encouraged to share their knowledge, in order to make informed Group Conscience decisions. We also encourage a thorough discussion, hearing all views. Our Group uses the 2022-2025 Al-Anon / Alateen Service Manual as a guide to answer Group questions.

The minutes on this site are for informational purposes only.

Members’ names are not included to maintain anonymity.

To request a copy of the full business meeting minutes, please send an email to newday7minutes@gmail.com. In your email please include the date of the minutes you are requesting in the subject field.

May 31, 2022

New Day 5AM Al-Anon Family Group Business Meeting. May 31, 2022.

Business Chair opened the meeting at 5:45AM.

Business Chair announced that she has served for 6 months and that her time is now up. The group thanked her for her service during this time. Our group is now in need of a Business Chairperson.

The next order of business is daily chairpersons for the month of June. Sunday- Member, Monday- Member, Tuesday- Member, Wednesday-Member, Thursday- Member, Friday-Member, Saturday-open.

Secretary then read minutes from April 30, 2022. Treasurer then asked for the opportunity to give treasurer report and to discuss allocation of funds. Business Chair declined this request and deferred to vote on the motion already put forth to have a full, 1 hour (if needed) business meeting on the last day of the month. The vote on this motion was 9 yes - 2 no - zero abstain. Business meetings in the future will start at 5AM on the last day of each month.

The Business Chairperson announced that any group member could request a group conscience meeting at any time. GR made a motion to have a business meeting on Thursday, June 2, and volunteered to chair as we do not have a business chair at this time. Motion was seconded and was unopposed. Business Chair then announced that there will be a Group Conscience meeting on Thursday. Purpose of this meeting is to give the Treasurer the opportunity to give his report and discuss allocation of funds.

A motion was made to extend our current meeting until 6:10. Motion was seconded and voted 7 yes – 0 no – 0 abstained. Meeting was closed at 6:10.

Respectfully submitted, Secretary.

March 14, 2022

Five AM New Day Al-Anon Family Group Business Meeting. March 14, 2022 Minutes.

Business Meeting Chair opened the meeting at 5:49AM. Secretary read the minutes from the March 7, 2022 meeting. Chairperson moved to go directly to today’s business which was to discuss whether the service position of treasurer is needed. GR read the Group Finance Section, page 158 from the service manual. We are now officially a Group and not just a phone meeting. As per the service manual, a treasurer is needed. A point was made regarding the need for a backup treasurer so that more than one name is on checks written. Another point was made about the length of time a person holding a service position has been active in the program. A third point was made about avoiding double headed management. Chairperson moved to extend the business meeting until 6:10AM. A motion was made and seconded to vote on the need for treasurer. The vote was yes-10, no-0, abstain 2. Chairperson moved to at least take care of this one item. We can discuss in the future a backup treasurer. Member has offered his service as treasurer. He is experienced in finance and has been active in the program for six years. Motion to appoint Member as treasurer. Motion seconded. We will vote at next business meeting on March 21, 2022. Chairperson lead us in Serenity Prayer. Meeting closed at 6:12AM.

Respectfully submitted, Secretary
